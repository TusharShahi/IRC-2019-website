Indian Rover challenge provides an opportunity to university students from India and around the world to gain practical experience in the design, testing, and implementation of prototype rovers. The second edition is organized by Manipal Academy of Higher Education in Manipal. For the first time competition of such a magnitude is happening in India as record numbers of teams are expected to participate. The competition would provide our sponsors exposure to dedicated teams from all over the world and would also create brand awareness due to heavy media coverage. Apart from that our sponsors would also get access to more than 20,000 Manipal Academy of Higher Education students. This is a great opportunity for your company to gain valuable exposure by sponsoring the event and to encourage young minds in their endeavor to build future Martian rovers.

Sponsorship is available in many forms. Purchased sponsorships, in-kind donation of product or service, and donations will all make this event possible, and inspire those attending and participating.
