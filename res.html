IRC 2018 was held at Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) from 6th to 8th January, 2018. The competition successfully tapped into the enthusiasm for designing and building the next generation of Mars Rovers and space robotics in the aspiring engineers. Team Mars Rover Manipal, from Manipal Institute of Technology, emerged as the winners of the first edition of competition with a grand total of 616 points, thereby doing honor to their title of ‘Asia’s Best Rover Design Team’. The runner-up of the competition was LU_Durbar of Leading University. The team from Bangladesh scored a total of 487 points, doing exceptionally well in the ‘Assistance Task’ as compared to the other teams. The second runner-up was another team from Bangladesh who go by the name of BRACU Mongol Tori of BRAC University. They scored 457.5 points in total and were the leading scorers in the ‘Surprise Task’, beating all other teams.

The competition was graced by the presence of some of the most renowned technocrats, scientists and professors coming from all parts of the country. Mr. Abhas Mitra, one of the most prominent astrophysicists of our country, was thrilled to see the amount of passion the teams had towards their work.

The main highlight of the competition was the brilliant display of hardwork which all the teams had put up within the time period of three days. The sheer excellence of their work was applauded by everyone.
